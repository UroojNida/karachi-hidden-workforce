About Street Vendors in Karachi

Discover their lives, challenges, and contributions to our city.

Who Are Street Vendors?

Street vendors in Karachi are an essential part of the informal economy. They sell food, clothes, and daily essentials to millions of people. Despite their role in making life convenient, they often face harassment, lack of legal recognition, and unstable incomes.

Why This Project?

This initiative aims to bring attention to the struggles faced by street vendors while showcasing their vital economic and social impact on Karachi.

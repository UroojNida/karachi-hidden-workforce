About Street Vendors in Karachi

Learn about their lives, challenges, and contributions.

Who Are They?

Street vendors in Karachi form a vital part of the informal economy. They sell food, clothes, and everyday essentials, making life easier for millions of residents. Despite their importance, they often face harassment, lack of legal recognition, and unstable incomes.

Why This Project?

This project aims to shed light on their struggles and highlight their economic and social significance for the city.

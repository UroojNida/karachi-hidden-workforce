Proposed Solutions

Strategies to improve the lives of Karachi's street vendors.

Policy Recommendations

  • Introduce a proper licensing system to legalize street vending.
  • Provide designated vending zones in urban areas.
  • Ensure social security and healthcare access.
  • Organize awareness campaigns for rights and regulations.

Community Initiatives

NGOs and local communities should work together to support street vendors through skill development programs, microfinance opportunities, and advocacy for fair treatment.

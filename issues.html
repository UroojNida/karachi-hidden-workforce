Issues Faced by Street Vendors

The hidden challenges of Karachi's informal economy workers.

Major Problems

  • Lack of legal protection and licenses.
  • Frequent harassment and bribes.
  • No access to healthcare or social security.
  • Poor working conditions and exposure to weather.
  • Eviction threats from authorities.

Impact of These Issues

These challenges make street vendors vulnerable and limit their ability to contribute effectively to the economy, despite being essential to Karachi's daily life.
